The Plate

First of its kind.
No logos. No clutter.
Just black.

The Blackout Plate is the first completely unbranded, all-black specialty plate in Arizona history. No cause logos, no organization names, no competing graphics. Just a clean black plate with white lettering and "ARIZONA" at the top. It's the plate you've been waiting for.

Standard Plate

$25/yr

ADOT assigns your unique plate number

$17 goes directly to Arizona children's charities through the Saguaros. $8 goes to Arizona's State Highway Fund. Renews annually with your registration.

Custom Vanity Plate

$50/yr

You choose up to 7 characters

Same $17 to charity. Pick any combination of letters and numbers — as long as it's available and under 7 characters, it's yours. Make it personal.

How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

01

Visit AZMVDNow.gov

Head to Arizona's MVD portal at AZMVDNow.gov and search for the "4AZ Kids" specialty plate under specialty plate options.

02

Choose Your Style

Select the standard plate ($25/yr) or customize it with your own 7-character combination ($50/yr).

03

Drive With Purpose

Your plate arrives and you're done. $17 goes to Arizona's children's charities every year when you renew. No extra steps.

The Impact

Every plate on the road
is a kid getting help.

The Saguaros have been fundraising for Arizona's children since 1987. Through annual grant programs, the organization distributes hundreds of thousands of dollars to 30+ nonprofits serving kids across the state. The Blackout Plate is a new way to keep that going — permanently.
